SteelLoadings
STEEL ORDER FLOW INTELLIGENCE
SteelLoadings gives steel market players early visibility into physical spot market flows — before they show up in port lineups or trade statistics.

We capture shipment orders at the moment they enter the market, typically 2–4 weeks before vessel loading. Each signal shows the origin port, destination, volume and loading window — enriched with the likely producer and cargo type, determined through a combination of proprietary algorithms and expert market knowledge.

Commodities covered:
Billets · Rebars · Wire rod · Beam · Sections · Rails · HRC · CRC · HDG · Plate · Slabs · Tubes

Built for market players who need to act early:
→ See where steel is moving before it moves
→ Identify supply pressure building on key trade routes
→ Know which mills are actively shipping and where

SteelLoadings doesn't tell you what happened last month. It tells you what the spot market is doing right now.
